- Bitwise Operators

band
- Bitwise AND
 Example :  
  5 & 3 = 1 (0101 & 0011 = 0001)

bor
- Bitwise OR
 Example :  
  5 | 3 = 7 (0101 | 0011 = 0111)

bnot
- Bitwise NOT
 Example :  
  ~5 = -6 ( ~0101 = 1010 + 1 = 1011 = -6)

bxor
- Bitwise XOR
 Example :  
  5 ^ 3 = 6 (0101 ^ 0011 = 0110)
