- Operators list :

+  : Addition
  Works with :
  - 2 numbers
  - 2 strings
  - 2 lists

-  : Subtraction
  Works with :
  - 2 numbers

*  : Multiplication
  Works with :
  - 2 numbers
  - 1 string and 1 number
  - 1 list and 1 number

/  : Division
  Works with :
  - 2 numbers

%  : Modulo
  Works with :
  - 2 numbers

** : Exponentiation
  Works with :
  - 2 numbers
  
= : Equality
  Works with :
  - 2 numbers
  - 2 strings
  - 2 lists
